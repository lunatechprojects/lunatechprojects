Industrial Preparation of Ammonia

The large scale production of ammonia is called the Haber Synthesis
Dry amonia and hydrogen gas are mixed in ratio of 1 to 3 respectively and heated in presence of a catalyst.
N2(g) + 3H2(g) = 2NH3(g)     (reversible reaction)
The heating is carried out at temperatures of 500oc and pressures of 200 atm. The catalyst is iron impregnated with alumina(aluminium oxide Al2O3).The reaction is reversible.

Uses of Ammonia

1.Ammonia is used to make nitrogenous fertilisers e.g ammonium sulphate and ammonium nitrate
2.In manufacture of important industrial compounds such a nitric acid, ammonium chloride and sodium carbonate.
3.Used in production of nylon.
4.Used in softening of temporary hard water.
5.Used as a cleaning agent.
